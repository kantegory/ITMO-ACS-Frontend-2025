This workout focuses on improving cardiovascular endurance through various exercises. Perfect for beginners who want to start their fitness journey or experienced athletes looking to maintain their cardio health.

Workout video placeholder

Video duration: 30 minutes
Equipment Needed:
  • Comfortable workout clothes
  • Running shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Exercise mat (optional)
Target Areas:
  • Cardiovascular system
  • Leg muscles
  • Core stability
Instructions:
Warm-up (5 minutes)
  • Light jogging in place: 2 minutes
  • Dynamic stretching: 3 minutes
  • Arm circles: 30 seconds each direction
  • Leg swings: 30 seconds each leg
Main Workout Circuit (Repeat 3 times)
  1. Jumping Jacks: 30 seconds
  2. High Knees: 30 seconds
  3. Rest: 15 seconds
  4. Butt Kicks: 30 seconds
  5. Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds
  6. Rest: 15 seconds
  7. Skater Jumps: 30 seconds
  8. Rest: 30 seconds between circuits
Cool Down (5 minutes)
  • Slow walking in place: 2 minutes
  • Static stretching: 3 minutes
  • Quad stretch: 30 seconds each leg
  • Hamstring stretch: 30 seconds each leg
  • Calf stretch: 30 seconds each leg
